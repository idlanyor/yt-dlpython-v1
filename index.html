API Endpoints

POST
/download/audio
Downloads the best quality audio from a YouTube URL and returns a public download link. Converts to MP3 format with 192kbps quality.
POST
/download/video
Downloads the best quality video from a YouTube URL in MP4 format. Automatically merges video and audio streams for optimal quality.
POST
/download/shorts
Specialized endpoint for downloading YouTube Shorts videos. Optimized for vertical video format and mobile content.
GET
/files/{filename}
Serves downloaded files directly. Use the filename from the download response to access your processed media files.
